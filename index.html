How CardioTwin
Works

From electrode to AI explanation in under a second. Four stages working together.

01
Capture ECG Signal
AD8232 sensor reads your heart's electrical activity via chest electrodes. Arduino Nano samples at 200Hz and streams raw values via USB serial.
HARDWARE
02
Process & Filter
Python bridge applies bandpass filtering, detects R-peaks, extracts HRV and RR intervals. Cleans noise from the raw analog signal.
PYTHON
03
AI Risk Analysis
JavaScript risk engine combines ECG features with lifestyle parameters. SHAP-style attribution shows which factor contributes most to your risk score.
RISK ENGINE
04
Gemini Explains
Google Gemini 1.5 Flash receives your ECG classification, risk score, symptoms, and lifestyle data — and explains everything in plain human language.
GEMINI AI
Capabilities

Everything You
Need to Monitor

From raw ECG waveform to personalized cardiac predictions.

♥
Live ECG Waveform
Real-time PQRST waveform display. Connects directly to your Arduino Nano + AD8232 sensor via WebSocket bridge. Falls back to realistic simulation without hardware.
⚡
Arrhythmia Detection
Automatically detects Normal Sinus Rhythm, Atrial Fibrillation, PVCs, Tachycardia, and Bradycardia from ECG morphology in real time.
%
Personalized Risk Score
Dynamic risk engine combining ECG features, HRV, sleep, exercise, stress, age, and comorbidities into a single percentage with SHAP attribution.
🔬
Symptom & Diagnosis Panel
9 symptom detectors running live. Clinical diagnosis panel with confidence scores. Auto-updates every 2 seconds with severity classification.
∿
What-If Simulation
Digital twin simulation engine: "What if I sleep 8 hours and stop smoking?" — computes new predicted risk and asks Gemini to explain the change narratively.
✦
AI Cardiac Assistant
Full conversational AI powered by Gemini 1.5 Flash. Answers questions about your specific ECG, risk factors, and lifestyle with context-aware responses.
◈
SQLite Patient Database
FastAPI backend stores all ECG sessions, vital readings, risk scores, and lifestyle history. View trends over time. Patient profile management.
⟳
Arduino Auto-Reconnect
WebSocket bridge handles disconnections gracefully. Auto-falls back to simulator if Arduino is unplugged. Reconnect with one click.
